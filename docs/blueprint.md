# **App Name**: FloraVision

## Core Features:

- Plant ID: Plant Identification: Identify plants and flowers using the phone's camera or uploaded photos via an AI tool.
- Health Analysis: Plant Health Analysis: Assess the health condition of plants and flowers from user-provided photos, providing insights on the health of the species via an AI tool.
- Care Tips: Care Tips Generation: Based on the plant's identification and health analysis, provide tailored advice for improving the plant's condition via an AI tool.
- Photo Upload: Photo Upload: Allow users to upload photos of plants and flowers for identification and analysis.
- Display Results: Display Results: Display the identified plant or flower, along with health assessment and care tips, in an easy-to-understand format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft green (#A7D1AB) to evoke nature and growth.
- Background color: Very light green (#F0FAF2) to give a fresh, clean backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted yellow (#D4AC0D) to highlight key information.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif with an elegant, intellectual feel
- Use line icons to represent plant characteristics and care actions.
- A clean, intuitive layout that prioritizes the photo and key information about plant identification, health assessment, and plant care recommendations.
